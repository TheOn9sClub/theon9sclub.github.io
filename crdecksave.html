Ever think of the current deck slots are not enough? This shortcut fixes it! You can save and copy decks easily by it! What does it do? -You can copy classic decks, crl decks, youtuber decks, etc. -You can save and copy your own decks (up to 10 decks). -You can join tournament by inserting tournament ID. How to open the menu? Go to clash royale and then click 'Cards', then 'Battle Decks', then 'Share Deck', then 'Link', then 'Shortcuts', then find 'Clash Royale Deck Changer'
